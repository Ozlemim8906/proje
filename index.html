What is CupCake?

It is a type of small cake prepared for serving to a person and is usually baked in a small thin paper or aluminum round cake tin. As with large cakes, decorations, sauces, etc. added to the cake. It is also common in cupcakes. The word "cupcake" was first mentioned in Eliza Leslie's Receipts cookbook in 1828. A standard cupcake contains the same basic ingredients as any other cupcake: sugar, butter, eggs, and flour.
